ENIMAL HISTORY

Eagles are known as birds of prey because they hunt animals as their food. They are similar to hawks, falcons, and vultures. Due to their strength and soaring flight, eagles held a special place in the human imagination. From ancient times the golden eagle has been a symbol of power.

Once upon a time, the animals of a jungle lived happily together. But one fine day, a ferocious and strong lion came to stay with them. Since the lion was big and strong, he started causing trouble and bullying the other animals. Fed up with this, the animals assembled together to discuss a way out of this.

Once upon a time, there lived a fierce tiger. He ruled over the forest and as his carnivore nature hunted his prey. One day, he was feeling very hungry. He looked around for prey and soon enough found prey and hunted it.
