KANKRIYA LAKE

Kankaria Lake is the largest lake in Ahmedabad
in the Indian state of Gujarat. It is located in
the south-eastern part of the city, in the Maninagar
area. It was completed in 1451 during the reign of Sultan
Qutb-ud-Din Ahmad Shah II though its origin is placed in
Chaulukya period sometimes.
